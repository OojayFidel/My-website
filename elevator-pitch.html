The Pitch

Hi, my name is Jonathan. I have a strong background in science, technical relations, and EdTech entrepreneurship, and I’ve recently expanded into social media management, where I help brands grow their digital presence.

My top three skills are analytical problem-solving, effective communication, and technology-driven project execution. These allow me to bridge scientific thinking, digital strategy, and real-world applications.

What sets me apart is my ability to adapt across industries, from managing technical challenges to building scalable education tools to driving audience engagement through social media. I combine precision, creativity, and business insight to create solutions that work.

For your organization, I’ll bring efficiency, innovation, and client-focused results, streamlining processes, boosting visibility, and creating impact.

I’d love the opportunity to connect further and explore how my background can contribute to your team’s goals.

Thank you for your time, and I look forward to continuing this conversation.

Script Beats

  • (0-10s) Intro: Name, hybrid role (social + tech).
  • (10-25s) The Problem: Many tech solutions lack human connection.
  • (25-40s) My Solution: I bridge that gap. Example of applying user empathy to a technical project.
  • (40-45s) The Ask: Seeking roles that value this blend of skills.
